Silver Jubliee Celebrations

About: Silver Jubilee Celebrations

Seshadri Rao Gudlavalleru Engineering College proudly celebrated its Silver Jubilee Celebrations from 16th to 18th February 2024, marking 25 years of academic excellence, innovation, and service. The three-day celebration brought together distinguished chief guests, dignitaries, faculty members, alumni, students, and guests to commemorate this remarkable milestone. The event featured inspiring keynote speeches, memorial lectures, technical sessions, award ceremonies, cultural performances, and the valedictory ceremony, making it a memorable occasion for everyone associated with the institution.

I had the privilege of serving as a Cultural Event Organizer throughout all three days of the celebrations. As a member of the organizing team, I actively coordinated and managed various cultural events, ensuring their smooth execution and contributing to the overall success of the celebrations. This opportunity enhanced my leadership, teamwork, communication, and event management skills while allowing me to contribute to one of the most prestigious events in our college.

The celebrations concluded with the Valedictory Ceremony, graced by Sri S. Abdul Nazeer, Hon'ble Governor of Andhra Pradesh, as the Chief Guest. During the closing ceremony, I had the honor of delivering the Vote of Thanks and joining the dignitaries, faculty members, and participants in the collective rendition of the National Anthem, bringing the Silver Jubilee Celebrations to a proud and patriotic conclusion.
